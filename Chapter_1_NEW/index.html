UNDERSTANDING A HYDROGEN ATOM

Before we begin our journey looking at how semiconductor devices work, we need to understand the inner workings of atoms. The Hydrogen Atom, due to its simple structure, has been extensively studied, and provides a good starting point to understand the fundamentals, before we move onto semiconductors such as Silicon.
